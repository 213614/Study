Seoul

officially known as the Seoul Special City, is the capital and largest metropolis of South Korea.
According to the 2020 census, Seoul has a population of 9.9 million people, and forms the heart of the Seoul Capital Area with the surrounding Incheon metropolis and Gyeonggi province.
